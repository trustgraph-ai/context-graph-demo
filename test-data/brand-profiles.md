# Brand Portfolio Overview

## Lumière Beauty

Lumière Beauty operates in the Cosmetics category with premium positioning. The brand currently runs 12 active campaigns and maintains a strong sentiment score of 0.87. Lumière Beauty merchandises in the Flagship Store NYC, leveraging the premium retail environment to showcase their products. The brand also activates via Pop-Up Experiences to create memorable customer moments.

## Nordic Trail

Nordic Trail is an Outdoor Apparel brand with sustainable positioning that resonates strongly with environmentally-conscious consumers. With 8 active campaigns and a sentiment score of 0.82, the brand has built solid market presence. Nordic Trail activates via Pop-Up Experiences to demonstrate product durability and sustainability credentials. The brand also promotes on Social Commerce to reach younger audiences with its environmental message.

## Velo Sport

Velo Sport competes in the Athletics category with performance-focused positioning. The brand runs 15 active campaigns, the most in our portfolio, though sentiment sits at 0.79 with room for improvement. Velo Sport sells through the Mobile Commerce App, capitalizing on the convenience factor for athletic purchases. The brand rewards loyal customers via the Loyalty Hub program with exclusive member benefits.

## Casa Verde

Casa Verde operates in the Home & Living category with artisanal positioning that emphasizes craftsmanship. Running 6 carefully curated campaigns, the brand achieves our highest sentiment score at 0.90. Casa Verde merchandises in the Flagship Store NYC where the tactile, handcrafted nature of products can be fully appreciated. The brand also promotes on Social Commerce to share the stories behind artisan creators.

## Artisan Coffee Co.

Artisan Coffee Co. is a Food & Beverage brand with community-focused positioning. The brand maintains 10 active campaigns and a sentiment score of 0.85. Artisan Coffee Co. activates via Pop-Up Experiences to bring the coffee culture to new neighborhoods and events. The brand rewards via the Loyalty Hub, offering points and exclusive blends to regular customers.
