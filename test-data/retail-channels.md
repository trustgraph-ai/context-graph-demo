# Retail Channel Performance Report

## Flagship Store NYC

The Flagship Store NYC is our premier physical retail channel, generating traffic of 48,000 visitors per month. The store achieves a conversion rate of 12.3% and maintains an exceptional experience score of 0.91. This location serves as the primary shopping destination for Active Families and Luxury Seekers who value the in-person retail experience. Lumière Beauty and Casa Verde both merchandise in this flagship location.

## Mobile Commerce App

The Mobile Commerce App represents our primary digital channel with impressive traffic of 1.2 million monthly users. The current conversion rate is 4.7% with an experience score of 0.78, indicating opportunities for optimization. Urban Millennials and Weekend Warriors are the primary consumer segments who shop via this channel. Velo Sport sells through the Mobile Commerce App as their main retail partnership.

## Pop-Up Experience

Pop-Up Experiences serve as our experiential retail channel, drawing traffic of 8,000 visitors per event. This channel achieves the highest conversion rate at 18.6% and the highest experience score of 0.95. Eco-Conscious Gen Z and Luxury Seekers are the segments that most actively experience these events. Nordic Trail, Lumière Beauty, and Artisan Coffee Co. all activate via Pop-Up Experiences to create immersive brand moments.

## Social Commerce

Social Commerce functions as our social retail channel with monthly traffic of 890,000 engaged users. The conversion rate of 3.2% is our lowest, though the experience score of 0.72 shows engagement potential. Urban Millennials discover products through Social Commerce, as do Eco-Conscious Gen Z consumers who use it as their primary discovery channel. Nordic Trail and Casa Verde both promote on Social Commerce to reach these audiences.

## Loyalty Hub

The Loyalty Hub operates as our omnichannel loyalty platform, engaging 340,000 members monthly. This channel achieves the highest conversion rate of 22.1% among existing members, with an experience score of 0.88. Active Families and Weekend Warriors are active members of the Loyalty Hub. Velo Sport and Artisan Coffee Co. both reward via the Loyalty Hub to drive repeat purchases and brand loyalty.
