# Consumer Segment Analysis Report

## Urban Millennials

Urban Millennials represent a segment of approximately 2.4 million consumers with an average monthly spend of $142. This segment demonstrates a loyalty score of 0.78 and is currently in the "Engaged" journey stage. They show strong affinity for Lumière Beauty and frequently purchase from Artisan Coffee Co. Urban Millennials primarily shop via the Mobile Commerce App and discover new products through Social Commerce channels.

## Active Families

The Active Families segment comprises 1.8 million consumers who spend an average of $218 per month. With a loyalty score of 0.85, this is one of our most committed segments, currently at the "Loyal" journey stage. Active Families have strong affinity for Nordic Trail outdoor apparel and regularly purchase from Velo Sport for athletic gear. They prefer shopping via the Flagship Store NYC and are active members of the Loyalty Hub program.

## Eco-Conscious Gen Z

Our fastest-growing segment, Eco-Conscious Gen Z, includes 3.1 million consumers with average monthly spending of $96. Their loyalty score of 0.62 reflects their "Exploring" journey stage as they evaluate brands against their values. This segment actively advocates for Nordic Trail due to its sustainable positioning. They also show affinity for Casa Verde's artisanal products and frequently visit Artisan Coffee Co. locations. Eco-Conscious Gen Z engages heavily through Pop-Up Experiences and discovers brands primarily through Social Commerce.

## Luxury Seekers

Luxury Seekers represent a premium segment of 890,000 consumers with the highest average spend at $384 per month. Their exceptional loyalty score of 0.91 and "Advocate" journey stage make them invaluable brand ambassadors. This segment is deeply loyal to Lumière Beauty and regularly purchases from Casa Verde. Luxury Seekers prefer the elevated experience of the Flagship Store NYC and actively participate in exclusive Pop-Up Experiences.

## Weekend Warriors

The Weekend Warriors segment includes 1.5 million consumers spending an average of $167 monthly. With a loyalty score of 0.73 and "Engaged" journey stage, they represent consistent but developing customer relationships. This segment has strong affinity for Velo Sport's performance athletics products and frequently visits Artisan Coffee Co. Weekend Warriors shop via the Mobile Commerce App and are members of the Loyalty Hub for rewards.
