# AI Agent Deployment Specifications

## Recommendation Agent

The Recommendation Agent specializes in Product Discovery capabilities. Operating with 94.2% accuracy and 120ms latency, this agent processes 2.1 million decisions per day. The Recommendation Agent recommends to Urban Millennials based on their mobile browsing patterns and also recommends to Eco-Conscious Gen Z aligned with their sustainability preferences. On the brand side, this agent curates products for Velo Sport and Casa Verde, optimizing product visibility and matching.

## Personalization Agent

The Personalization Agent focuses on Experience Tailoring capabilities. With 91.8% accuracy and industry-leading 85ms latency, it handles 890,000 decisions daily. This agent personalizes for Luxury Seekers, delivering the white-glove digital experience this segment expects, and personalizes for Active Families with family-friendly recommendations. The Personalization Agent tailors experience at the Flagship Store NYC through in-store digital touchpoints and tailors experience at the Mobile Commerce App through dynamic content personalization.

## Campaign Orchestrator

The Campaign Orchestrator provides Brand Activation capabilities across channels. Operating at 88.5% accuracy with 200ms latency, it manages 340,000 campaign decisions per day. This agent orchestrates campaigns for Lumière Beauty, Nordic Trail, and Artisan Coffee Co., coordinating messaging and timing across touchpoints. The Campaign Orchestrator deploys campaigns at Pop-Up Experiences for immersive activations and deploys campaigns at Social Commerce for viral engagement.

## Sentiment Analyst

The Sentiment Analyst delivers Brand Perception capabilities with the highest accuracy in our agent portfolio at 96.1%. With 150ms latency, it processes 1.5 million sentiment decisions daily. This agent monitors sentiment of Urban Millennials and monitors sentiment of Eco-Conscious Gen Z, tracking these influential segments' brand perceptions. The Sentiment Analyst analyzes perception of Lumière Beauty to protect premium positioning and analyzes perception of Velo Sport to identify improvement opportunities.

## Journey Optimizer

The Journey Optimizer provides Path Optimization capabilities to maximize conversion. With 89.7% accuracy and 180ms latency, it handles 560,000 optimization decisions per day. This agent optimizes journey for Active Families, streamlining their multi-step purchase paths, and optimizes journey for Weekend Warriors to capture weekend shopping intent. The Journey Optimizer optimizes flow at the Flagship Store NYC for in-store navigation and optimizes flow at the Loyalty Hub to maximize reward redemption and repeat visits.
